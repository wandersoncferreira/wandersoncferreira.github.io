focus

focus is the ability to carefully pay attention to something such as a task or a person. it requires a lot of concentration.

also, in the world of sport psycology, focus is a critical skill that must be mastered by all athletes that desire to exceed in their role. for my particular scenario at the moment, which is running, there are some key moments that i need to focus on the task at hand:

  1. run races regularly
  2. keep a long run every week
  3. do the workouts regularly

it is very important to keep myself concentrated and present in the moment when i am realizing these activities. therefore, i do not listen to music, podcasts, etc. i try to minimize all distractions that i can control. the main ideal of this cleaner environment is to be able to focus on the signals that my body provides me at every step.

i already noticed that keeping ourselves focused is a arduous task. it is difficult to keep concentrate in a task of 1 or 2hrs of duration. however, there are long term objectives that requires daily focus such as keeping ourself following the diet plan to reach the desired body composition to perform the way we desire at some event or circunstance.

therefore, for these situations it is important to rely on short term goals to keep us in the correct track:

  1. weekly mileage
    • Me at 2024-08-26: want to reach 80km per week
  2. monthly mileage
  3. more consistent with strength training and stretching
